Our Mission

Our Mission is simple, to create beautiful gardens which will change your world.

Whether this is your place of work, your home or your community; we believe all spaces have the potential to be magical.
Our team of experienced Horticulturists can transform both residential and commerical areas, by including inspirational componenents such as sensory, ornamental or edible planting, woodland creations or urban escapes.

We are part artist, part scientist and the rest is hard work.

Award-Winning Gardens:

2020
Best Private Garden under €30,000

ALCA Landscape Awards

This development aimed to take advantage of the warm climate found on the Hugh Town Island. Using water as the main focal point, rose & dahlia beds were designed for a summer burst of color. Additionally, a tropical bed and rockery were planted to achieve a mediterranean style.
2019
Best Commercial Design under €50,000

Glasca Garden Awards

Visted by thousands each year, Waymar park underwent a re-design and development project in 2019. The aim was to improve biodiversity and provide year-round interest. Approximately 5000 native trees were planted during this time, 600 shrub varieties and 10 meadow plots.
2017
Best Urban design under €30,000

ALCA Lanscape Awards

Proving that small gardens can be magicial, a 30 sqm plot was transformed into a urban paradise. A soft palette of blues, purples and pinks was used to create a sense of space throughout the area, and this was combined with a mix of tree ferns and low growing alpines.

Our Recent Creations:

August, 2020

Willow Park, Ballincolig

Featuring Prunus 'Asano' as a centerpiece, the avenue is underplanted with pink palette summer flowering perennials. several Asian temples are constructed along the grounds, using local reclaimed wood and recycled plastic.
March,2020

Café Nervosa, Carrigaline

Incorporating nature into hospitality, Houseplants take center-stage inside, transforming the area into a lively jungle. Outside, contemporary pots take the focus, planting with colorful ornamental grasses and everygreen shrubs.
October, 2019

Carrig Business Park

A disused area was transformed into an outdoor social space using specimen trees, ornamental grasses and an area of seasonal color. A lounge area, created from recycled plastic is directly surrounded by a wildflowers.
July, 2019

Willis Eco-Park, Bandon

Traditional Dwellings were constructed, equipped with vertical walls and green roofing. A vegetable garden was created which included herbs, vegetables and fruit trees. For weed management, coco fiber matting was laid.
February, 2019

Keswick Gardens, Tramore

Hostas, Bamboo and Acer tree gardens were constructed to create an Asian style garden. Additionally a sunken moss garden was built, which replaced an overgrown disused area with low-maintenance Bryophyta.
January, 2019

Evergreen Terrace, Blackrock

Modern and refined, The Evergreen Terrace design was inspired by L.A luxury gardens and Italian planting. Lighting is used to compliment silver-leafed Olea europaea and over-hanging plants. Low maintenance with high impact.
