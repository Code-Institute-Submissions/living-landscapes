Our story

Living Landscapes is an award winning garden design company based in County Cork, Munster.

We are Plant-first landscapers, meaning that we put emphasis on the planting; making sure that your garden will match your maintenace requirements and look beautiful in every season of the year.

We are part artist, part scientist and the rest is hard work.
